Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Built for people who actually use AI every day — not people who demo it at conferences.

⚡

Real-time streaming

Watch your AI think in real time. Tokens flow in as they're generated — no staring at a spinner waiting for a wall of text.

🌿

Conversation branching

Edit any message and fork the conversation. Explore different paths without losing your place. Navigate branches with arrow keys.

📑

Topic sidebar

Jump between conversations instantly. See what's active, stay organized across projects. Your topics, always one click away.

🔀

Switch models on the fly

Swap between Claude, GPT, Gemini, and more mid-conversation. Drag to reorder your favorites. The right model for every task.

📊

See your context window

A gorgeous visual grid showing exactly what's eating your token budget. Never fly blind again — know what your AI sees.

🔧

Tool calls, visible

See every tool your AI uses: file edits, web searches, code execution. Collapsible cards with timing. Full transparency into every action.

🧠

Thinking traces

Peek inside your AI's reasoning process with expandable thinking panels. Understand not just what it says, but how it got there.

⌨️

Command palette

Cmd+K for everything. Quick actions, model switching, session commands. If you've used Raycast or VS Code, you'll feel right at home.

🎨

Dark & light mode

Warm dark theme with gold accents. Clean light mode. Both gorgeous. Respects your system preference, or toggle whenever you want.

✨

Just works

Auto-reads your gateway token. npm install and npm run dev. Done. Two commands and you're in.
